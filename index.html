Come And Visit Roxas Isabela

"Roxas is a charming yet prosperous municipality in Isabela. It has a large, but organized public market that sells the everyday wet and dry goods and souvenirs. It's also known for the Borubor Falls, an underrated destination in Isabela. Other places of interest include parks such as the Forest Park, Children's Park, and Roxas Municipal Park."

Pinakbet Festival

"Locals refer to Roxas as the “Pinakbet Bowl of the North” due to its large-scale production of vegetables."

Pagay Festival

Pagay Festival is well-known festival in Roxas celebrated every fourth of July.

Freedom Park

Freedom Park is like a transportation hub in Roxas. Almost all of public utility vehicle is located here.

Xentro_Mall_Roxas

Xentro_Mall_Roxas is a shopping mall in Roxas where we can buy almost all of our neccisties. Located at Don Mariano, Marcos Avenue, Roxas City, Isabela.

Lado del Rio Resort

Lado del Rio Resort is one of the most popular family resorts in Roxas. Its Located at National Highway, San Placido in Roxas

Borubor Falls

Borubor Falls is located at Sinamar, Roxas, Isabela.
